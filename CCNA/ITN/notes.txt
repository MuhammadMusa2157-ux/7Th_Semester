Here's a breakdown of the terms:
Port: A physical connector or outlet on a device where a cable is plugged in. 
Interface: A specialized port on a networking device that serves as the point of interaction between that device and an individual network. 
NIC (Network Interface Card): A hardware component (like a card or chip) that provides the physical connection for a device to join a network.
